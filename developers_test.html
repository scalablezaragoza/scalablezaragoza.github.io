Frequently asked questions

What is the fee to use Scalable API?
Free! We only charge process fee when your customers make a purchase.
How long does it take to generate a product page from an image or a video clip?
Depending on the assets and the product type, usually a product page is generated with 20 minutes.
Do I need to process payment?
No. Scalable API takes care of the payment process.
What if the customers need to report an order issue?
Scalable takes care of the customer support.
How long does it take to process an order?
Customers will receive an order confirmation email with 24 hours.
How long before the order is shipped?
Depending on the item, the order usually ships within 5 to 7 business days.
